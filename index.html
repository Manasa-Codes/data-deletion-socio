Data Deletion Policy

This page provides instructions for users of our app to request deletion of their data.

If you wish to delete your account and the associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact us via email at support@socio-app.com with your request.
  2. Alternatively, visit the following URL to request deletion of your data:
    • Delete My Data (this link will process your data deletion request).

If you have any questions or concerns about data deletion, please contact our support team at support@socio-app.com.
